DOCKIT

DockIt is a CRM created for the reconstruction and remodeling industry. Its primary function is to organize an estimator's assignments and any associated required tasks as they move through the full reconstruction cycle.

After my client realized that their current technological applications nurtured all employees except the primary salespeople for their company, my client sought the creation of a CRM that would address these downfalls. DockIt's ultimate goal is to provide estimators with all tools necessary to enhance customer service levels, adhere to program requirements, and improve sales. DockIt also has built-in features that encourage sales management and performance review.

I was the sole developer for this project and completed it in under 2 weeks.
